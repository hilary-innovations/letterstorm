Privacy Policy

Scramblox collects no personal data. This policy explains what we do (and don't do) with your information.

1. Who We Are

Scramblox is an independent word puzzle game. Bundle ID: com.scramblox.words.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We collect zero personal information of any kind.

  • No names, emails, or contact info
  • No location or GPS
  • No device IDs or ad IDs
  • No photos, camera, or microphone
  • No financial data
  • No cross-app tracking

3. Local Storage Only

Game progress (coins, stars, scores, settings) is saved locally on your device using SharedPreferences. It never leaves your device.

🔒 No servers. No cloud. Nothing transmitted anywhere.

4. Internet Permission

Declared as a Flutter standard requirement. Not used to send or receive data.

5. Third-Party SDKs

None. No analytics, ads, crash reporters, or trackers of any kind.

6. Children (COPPA)

Safe for all ages including children under 13. No data collected from anyone.

7. GDPR

No personal data collected — fully GDPR compliant by design.

8. Delete Your Data

Uninstall the app. All local data is removed. No action needed from us.
