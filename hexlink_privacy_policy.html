THE SHORT VERSION: HexLink COLLECTS NOTHING.

No accounts. No analytics. No ads. No tracking. No internet connection required. Your data never leaves your device — because we never collect any.

1. Information We Collect

None. HexLink does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data of any kind. The game contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporters, and makes no network requests.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

HexLink saves only your game progress locally on your device:

  • Levels completed and star ratings earned
  • Sound, music, and vibration preferences

This data is stored using Android's standard app storage, never leaves your device, and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

3. Permissions

The only permission HexLink may use is VIBRATE, for optional haptic feedback. It can be turned off in Settings at any time. The app requests no access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or any other sensitive data.

4. Children's Privacy

HexLink is suitable for all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Because the app collects no data whatsoever, no information about children — or anyone else — is ever gathered, processed, or shared.

5. Third-Party Services

HexLink contains no third-party services, no advertising networks, no social media integrations, and no external links. It is a fully self-contained, offline game.

6. Changes To This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date. Since the app collects no data, any future changes are expected to remain just as simple.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out through the developer contact listed on the app's Google Play Store page.
